Last updated: 10 July 2026.

BareChart is run by a single private trader. This page explains what data is collected when you visit and why.

## Analytics

This site uses Google Analytics to understand overall traffic — pages visited, approximate location (country/city level, from IP), device and browser type, and how visitors found the site. This is aggregate usage data, not tied to your name or identity. Google Analytics sets cookies to do this; you can block them with your browser's cookie settings or a tracking-blocker extension without affecting your ability to use the site.

## Affiliate links

Some links on this site (marked or routed through `/go/`) are affiliate links — if you sign up with a firm or broker through one, I may earn a commission at no extra cost to you. Clicking one of these links takes you to the partner's site, which will set its own cookies to track that the referral came from here. I don't control what those third-party sites do with your data once you're there — check their own privacy policy for that.

## What this site doesn't do

No account system, no login, no comments, no newsletter signup, no payment processing on this site directly. No personal data is sold to third parties.

## Your choices

You can block or delete cookies at any time through your browser settings. Doing so may affect analytics accuracy but won't break any page on this site.
