Find the right funded account for how you trade. Filter by challenge cost,
drawdown limits, profit targets, payout percentage, and whether EAs and
algo trading are permitted.

Use the **EA/Algo** toggle if you run automated strategies — not all firms allow them.

New to prop trading? A funded account lets you trade a firm's capital and keep a
percentage of profits. Pass a challenge, get funded, earn from there.

*Data is reviewed regularly but rules change — always verify with the firm before purchasing.*
